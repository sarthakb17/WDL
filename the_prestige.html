Information of the movie :

The Prestige is a 2006 psychological thriller film directed by Christopher Nolan, co-written with Jonathan Nolan, and based on the 1995 novel by Christopher Priest. The movie stars Hugh Jackman and Christian Bale as rival stage magicians, Robert Angier and Alfred Borden, in Victorian-era London.

The plot revolves around their intense rivalry as both try to outdo each other in creating the ultimate stage illusion, leading to a dark obsession with sacrifice, deception, and the price of greatness. The supporting cast includes Michael Caine, Scarlett Johansson, Rebecca Hall, Andy Serkis, and David Bowie as the enigmatic Nikola Tesla.

Released on October 20, 2006, the film received positive reviews and was praised for its direction, storytelling, and performances. It grossed $109 million worldwide on a $40 million budget and earned Academy Award nominations for Best Art Direction and Best Cinematography.
