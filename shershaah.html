Information of the movie :

Shershaah is a 2021 Indian Hindi-language biographical war film based on the life of Captain Vikram Batra, who was posthumously awarded the Param Vir Chakra for his actions during the Kargil War. The film marks the Hindi directorial debut of Vishnuvardhan and was written by Sandeep Shrivastava.

The movie stars Sidharth Malhotra in a dual role as Vikram Batra and his twin brother Vishal, alongside Kiara Advani as Dimple Cheema, Vikram’s fiancée. Produced by Dharma Productions and Kaash Entertainment, the film was originally set for a theatrical release but was later premiered digitally on Amazon Prime Video due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Shershaah received widespread critical acclaim for its performances, music, cinematography, and emotional depth. The portrayal of Vikram Batra’s bravery and personal life struck a chord with the audience. It went on to become one of the most-watched Indian films on Amazon Prime Video.
