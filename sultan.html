Information of the movie :

Sultan is a 2016 Indian Hindi-language sports drama film directed by Ali Abbas Zafar and produced by Aditya Chopra under the banner of Yash Raj Films. The movie features Salman Khan in the lead role as Sultan Ali Khan, a fictional wrestler from Haryana, alongside Anushka Sharma, Amit Sadh, and Randeep Hooda.

The story revolves around Sultan's journey from being a local wrestling champion to a global star and the personal sacrifices that come with fame. The film showcases his rise, fall, and redemption as he struggles to reclaim his life and career.

The film's production began in October 2015, and it was released on July 6, 2016, coinciding with the festival of Eid. It was praised for its performances, especially Salman Khan’s transformation, emotional depth, and action sequences. With a global gross of over ₹623 crore, it became one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time.
